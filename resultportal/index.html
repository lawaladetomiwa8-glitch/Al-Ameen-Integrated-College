Student Result Portal

Check your academic results securely using your unique Student ID number.

How It Works

Checking Your Results

🔑

Your Student ID

Every student is assigned a unique ID number at Al-Ameen. You will need this ID to access your results. Keep it safe and confidential.

📋

Enter Your ID

In the portal below, enter your Student ID exactly as provided by the school. IDs are case-sensitive — enter them carefully.

📊

View Your Results

Your academic results for the current or most recent term will be displayed. Results are updated by school administration after every term.

⚠️

Forgotten your Student ID? Please do not share your Student ID with anyone. If you have misplaced or forgotten your ID, kindly contact the school office directly at +234 8132135930 or +234 9124231021, or send an email to alameenintegratedcollege@gmail.com. Results are personal and cannot be accessed without the correct ID.

ℹ️
Important Notice:

Results are typically published within one week after the end of each term. If you have submitted your Student ID correctly and results are not showing, it may be because results for the current term have not yet been published. Please check back later or contact the school.

🔗

Open Result Portal

The result portal is best accessed via the link below. Click the button to open your results in a secure window.
